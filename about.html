Introduction

Hello, I am Moises Danielle Tulinao, a BSIT student at the University of Cebu. Welcome to my personal webpage where I share my journey, background, and the reasons behind my choices.

Why Information Technology?

At first, I planned to become a marine engineer, inspired by my father who sailed the seas. I took the STEM strand in senior high school to pursue this dream.

However, after graduating, my parents strongly discouraged this path. Though disappointed, I adapted and searched for other opportunities. I discovered IT as one of the most in-demand and well-paid fields.

Choosing BSIT wasn’t part of the original plan, but now I’m embracing it. With effort, dedication, and passion, I believe I can succeed and build a fulfilling career in this industry.
